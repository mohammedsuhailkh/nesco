Who we really are &
why choose us

Located in Manickamangalam, Kalady in Ernakulam District, we soon converted a little land into a shiny new facility and from it, eagerly worked towards our goal; to make a family of ice creams that would be second to none in their taste and creamy texture. For the Last two decade, we are obsessed with creating the most heavenly ice cream, using the natural flavors of vanilla, dark chocolate, roasted nuts and fresh seasonal fruits. Under the able guidance of our MD, Mr Manoj Ramakrishnan , we are the first and only company who give delicious ice-creams in NATURAL FRUIT CONTAINERS. We have also diversified business interest in flavor extractions & exports of fruit shell containers to Europe & US markets.
